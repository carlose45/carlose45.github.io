Intro

Carlos is a systematic thinker. From a very young age he has been passionate about research and technological development. He is cooperative and collaborated, oriented to follow procedures. Cautious in approaching problems and high-level experience in well strategized decisions. Proficient at quickly learning new procedures and taking ownership of diverse projects.

Carlos is a professional in Systems Engineering and with a M.Eng. in Data Analysis and Process Improvement, he has focused on service-oriented architectures with the development of APIs and integration of multiple technologies to offer innovative software products in line with business processes.

He is a passionate traveler and SciFy fan.
